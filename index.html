Kitchen Room Design

Modern kitchen design consists of sleek, angular, and simple designs featuring little hardware, flush doors, and flat surfaces. They are usually of geometric forms and they contain little or no ornamentation or adornments. The kitchen designs, modern or traditional, have some distinctive features themselves. Notice how the gold accents of the vintage cabinet-pulls ties in with the contemporary pendant lights above this dining table. The decor unites the two spaces together.

Family Room Design

Nothing says bright and well-lit like this beautiful room. The living space comprises a couch and chairs in soft grey shades, counterbalanced by the table and shelves in warm brown. The room is adjacent to a white minimalist kitchen with modern black stools and spot light fixtures in the ceiling.
